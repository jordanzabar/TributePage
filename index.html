Kawhi Leonard

A Canadian Basketball Hero

Kawhi Leonard during one of his games as a Toronto Raptor.

Here's a timeline of Kawhi Leonard's Life:

  • 1991 - Born in Los Angeles, California
  • 2009 - Attended Canyon Springs High School in Moreno Valley, California before transfering to Martin Luther King High School for his junior year.
  • 2010 - Went to San Diego State University where he averaged 12.7 points and 9.9 rebounds per game.
  • 2011 - Drafted by the San Antonio Spurs with the 15th overall pick.
  • 2014 - Won the NBA Championship with the San Antonio Spurs where he was awarded Finals MVP. He averaged 17.8 points on 61% shooting.
  • 2015 - Won Defensive Player of the Year award.
  • 2016 - Was selected to his first All Star game.
  • 2017 - Had a career high in all categories and was selected to his second First All NBA team.
  • 2018 - Suffered a quadriceps injury and had conflict with the San Antonio Spurs on how they handled his injury. Ultimately told the team he wanted to be traded.
  • 2019 - Kawhi Leonard was traded to the Toronto Raptors in the offseason. He carried the team on his back to win the franchise's first NBA Championship. He was awared Finals MVP.
  • 2019 - Decided to sign with the Los Angeles Clippers to be closer to home and his family.
